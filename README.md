### 👨‍💻 About Me 👨‍💻

I am an **Identity & Access Management (IAM) Specialist** and **Software Engineer** with a Master’s in Computer Information Science. My work sits at the intersection of security, automation, and infrastructure.

* 🔭 **Core Focus:** Designing secure identity frameworks (Okta, Entra ID) and automating complex user lifecycles.
  
* 🛡️ **Philosophy:** **Secure by design, frictionless by default.** I implement security controls that enable speed rather than creating bottlenecks.
